Platform features

Everything employability, in one place

REDI brings together academic data, career tools, and verified documents into a single employability score for every student.

ERI scoring engine

A weighted algorithm combining 9 parameters including grades, internships, projects, certifications, and attendance into one employability score.

AI career coach

A built-in advisor that analyzes each student's profile and gives personalized career guidance and recommendations.

Verified documents

Transcripts, certificates, and reports are uploaded, verified by teachers, and available as secure downloadable PDFs.

Career passport

Students build a complete profile with photo, bio, skills, internships, and certifications visible to employers searching for talent.

Talent search

Employers filter candidates by school, program, department, and ERI score to find the right graduates for internships and jobs.

Attendance & results

Teachers record attendance, assignments, quizzes, and observations directly, automatically updating each student's employability score.

How it works

From classroom to career, in four steps

1

Institution setup

Admin creates schools, programs, courses, and accounts for students, teachers, and employers.

2

Data collection

Teachers record grades, attendance, and observations. Students upload documents and certifications.

3

ERI computation

The engine generates an updated employability score from 0-100% based on 9 weighted parameters.

4

Talent matching

Employers search verified profiles by ERI score, school, and program to find qualified candidates.
