Platform features

Everything employability, in one place

REDI brings together academic data, career tools, and verified documents into a single employability score.

ERI scoring engine

A weighted algorithm combining grades, internships, projects, certifications, and attendance into one employability score.

AI career coach

A built-in advisor that analyzes each student's profile and gives personalized career guidance, by text or voice.

Verified documents

Transcripts, certificates, and reports are uploaded, verified, and available as secure downloadable PDFs.

Career passport

Students build a complete profile with photo, bio, skills, and LinkedIn — visible to employers searching for talent.

Talent search

Employers filter candidates by field, department, and ERI score to find the right graduates faster.

Attendance tracking

Teachers record attendance and results directly, automatically updating each student's employability score.
