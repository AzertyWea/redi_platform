FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate via Mobile Money (Orange Money, MTN), bank transfer, or visit our office directly. Online card payments will be available soon.

Can I volunteer with SERA?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. Contact us with your skills and availability, and we will match you with a program.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

As a registered non-profit, we can provide donation receipts. Tax deductibility depends on your country's regulations. Contact us for details.

How do I know my donation is used well?

We publish annual financial reports and welcome on-site visits. Every donation is tracked and allocated according to our transparency policy.
