About SERA Foundation

We are a registered non-profit humanitarian organization committed to transforming lives across Africa through education, compassion, and community-driven action.

Who We Are

A Organization Built on Compassion

SERA Foundation was established in 2018 with a clear purpose: to stand beside Africa's most vulnerable communities and create pathways to a better life. Founded by a group of humanitarian workers, educators, and community leaders, we have grown from a small initiative to a recognized non-profit operating across multiple countries.

We believe that every child deserves an education, every family deserves food and shelter, and every community deserves access to basic healthcare. These are not privileges — they are fundamental human rights.

Our approach is community-driven. We work hand-in-hand with local leaders, families, and volunteers to ensure our programs address real needs with sustainable solutions.

Why We Exist

The Challenges We Fight Against

Millions of children and families across Africa face daily struggles that no human being should endure. Here is why our work matters.

🎓

Education Inequality

Over 100 million children in Sub-Saharan Africa lack access to basic education. Poverty, distance to schools, and lack of resources keep millions of children out of the classroom every day.

💰

Poverty Challenges

Africa remains home to the world's poorest populations. Millions of families survive on less than $1.90 a day, struggling to afford food, shelter, and basic necessities for their children.

💧

Lack of Basic Resources

Access to clean water, sanitation, and healthcare remains a luxury for many rural communities. preventable diseases claim thousands of young lives every year due to inadequate medical support.

🤝

Need for Community Support

Orphans, abandoned children, and displaced families need organized support systems. Without community-driven intervention, these vulnerable groups face isolation and hopelessness.
