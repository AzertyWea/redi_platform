Amara's Journey to Becoming a Doctor

Education Success

From Orphan to Honor Student

Amara lost both her parents to illness when she was just 7 years old. Left in the care of her elderly grandmother, she had no prospect of ever attending school. The nearest classroom was a 3-hour walk away, and there was no money for uniforms, books, or fees.

When SERA Foundation reached her village in 2020, Amara was enrolled in our Education Support Program. Today, at age 14, she is the top student in her class and dreams of becoming a doctor so she can help her community.

Before

No school access, malnourished, no medical care, living in extreme poverty.

After

Top student, healthy, full scholarship, aspiring doctor with hope for the future.

Amara K. Student, Age 14 — Dakar Region, Senegal
